Combined Fast Intentions, Mosque Renovation & Fiqh Symposium Rulings - May 26, 2026

📅 2026-05-26 🕐 16:00

Overview

This session of the Majma' al-Fatawa bi'l-Hind weaves together classical and contemporary Shafi'i fiqh. It opens with a question on combining the fast of the Day of Arafah with a make-up (qada') of Ramadan, moves into a detailed fatwa by 'Abd al-Rahman al-Mashhur on the renovation of an ancient mosque in Shibam (Hadramawt) including the rules of selling worn-out mats and woodwork, and then works systematically through Resolutions 71–75 of the 7th–17th Symposium of the Islamic Fiqh Academy held at Madrasa Sheikh 'Ali al-Muttaqi (Burhanpur, India): environmental pollution, sex education in schools, modern medical treatments for the fasting person, the starting point of travel distance, and dual-residence rulings for those working away from home. The session closes with brief notes on protection from the evil eye through adhkar and a clarification on the three-day rule for grazing animals in zakat.
